It's a Simple Spell But Quite Unbreakable

Based on the Forward chaining (or forward reasoning) implementation strategy for expert systems, the CMM-B hosts an inference engine that is able to logically link various conditions with associated consequences in between given statements. To provide in the very end a link between your Hypothesis and the provided fact database.
